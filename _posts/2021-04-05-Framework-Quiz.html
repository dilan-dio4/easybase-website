Take this quiz for a custom ranking of these JavaScript framework/libraries. permalink: best-javascript-framework-library-quiz/ --- Front-end frameworks have become incredibly popular over the last decade and don't appear to be slowing down any time soon. There are many options out there when it comes to choosing a framework/library for your next project. The differences between these frameworks may seem subtle at first glance, but they could have consequences later on. This is especially important if you plan on putting significant resources into your next project. You certainly want to set yourself up for success. Choosing the proper method will absolutely pay off in the long term.

React, React Native, Angular, Vue.js, Ember.js and Backbone.js are all options that could be the perfect fit for you, depending on your specific use case. These libraries work fantastic with serverless architecture which allows developers to implement production-ready modules into their applications, such as user authentication, database, and cloud functions.
